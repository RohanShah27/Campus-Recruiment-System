APSIT

A. P. Shah Institute of Technology known as APSIT is a Private Engineering College located in Kasarvadavali, Thane district of Maharashtra state in India. It was established in 2014, and is managed by the Parshvanath Charitable Trust. It is a Jain Religious Minority College (i.e., 51% of all seats are reserved for students from the Jain Religious Minority Community). It is affiliated to the University of Mumbai (a public university funded by the state government of Maharashtra), is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) of the Government of India, and is recognised by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) of the state Government of Maharashtra.
